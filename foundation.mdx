Welcome to the technical and operational blueprint for **Superadmission**.

Currently, the student acts as the manual integration layer across dozens of parallel admission systems (JoSAA, MCC, State CETs). They repeatedly verify their identity, submit identical documents to different portals, and navigate colliding deadlines. Superadmission is designed to sit _above_ these systems, acting as a unified public infrastructure layer.

<Note>
  **Project Status** \
  The MVP is currently in active development (focused initially on JEE Mains and Advanced) and is not yet a deployed government platform. This documentation outlines the proposed architecture and operational flows.
</Note>

## Core Infrastructure

Explore the foundational pillars designed to eliminate friction for the student while granting absolute governance to counselling authorities.

## Why Superadmission?

The system is designed to preserve the existing rules while upgrading the rails they run on.

- **Authorities** keep 100% control over reservations, cutoffs, and seat mapping.
- **Exams** like JEE and NEET still determine the ranks without interference.
- **The Infrastructure** simply handles the friction: data transfer, document routing, and deadline coordination.
