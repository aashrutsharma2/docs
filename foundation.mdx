A student with a JEE Main rank participates in JoSAA, their state CET, and one or two institutional counsellings — **simultaneously, with no coordination between any of them**. Separate accounts. Same documents uploaded three times. Deadlines that collide. Acceptance decisions made in one system with no visibility into the others.

Superadmission sits above these systems as a **shared coordination layer** — not a replacement for any of them — so students stop carrying that burden manually.

## The shift being proposed

JoSAA allocates IIT and NIT seats. MCC allocates AIQ medical seats. State CETs allocate within their jurisdiction. **None of this changes.** What changes is the layer the student interacts with before each of those systems — and the coordination between them.

| Today | Proposed |
| --- | --- |
| New account per counselling | One Aadhaar-linked profile, created once |
| Same documents uploaded 4.1 times per cycle on average | Fetched from DigiLocker once, verified once, accepted everywhere |
| Deadlines tracked manually across portals | Unified deadline view with alerts at 72h, 24h, and 6h |
| Allotment status checked per portal | Single dashboard, side-by-side comparison |
| Guidance from paid consultants (Rs 5,000 to Rs 2 lakh) | PraveshAI at every decision point, available to every student |

The figures above come from approximately **2,000 student journeys** tracked through CollegeCult operations — not projections.

## India Stack alignment

The proposed architecture is designed to sit on infrastructure that already exists.

| Component | Role |
| --- | --- |
| **Aadhaar** | Identity layer. Optional e-KYC and OTP-based authentication at profile creation. |
| **DigiLocker** | Document layer. Mark sheets and rank cards fetched from issuing authorities with student consent. |
| **UPI** | Payment layer. All registration and acceptance fees across every counselling, one interface. |
| **APAAR** | Academic identity. Persistent student record compatible with the Academic Bank of Credits under NEP 2020. |

This follows the same structural pattern as UPI for payments and ONDC for commerce — a shared protocol layer above existing independent actors, with no actor replaced.

## Scale

**23 lakh students** appeared for JEE Main in 2024. **24 lakh** for NEET UG. Average 2.3 counselling registrations per student per cycle. Average 4.1 document submissions of the same documents.

NEP 2020 targets 50% Gross Enrolment Ratio by 2035, against 28.4% today. Volume into this process grows every year. The coordination infrastructure has not.
