SuperAdmission gives your admissions team a shared brain. Connect your CRM, pull live applicant signals, and surface the decisions that actually move yield — without touching a spreadsheet.

It's not a dashboard you check. It's infrastructure that works while you're in committee.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your CRM">
    Plug in Slate, Salesforce, or Technolutions via OAuth or a direct API key. We pull your pipeline, normalize it, and never write back without your permission.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define your cohort">
    Set filters — program, geography, aid tier, test score band — and SuperAdmission builds a live cohort you can interrogate in plain English.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Surface the signals that matter">
    Visit frequency, email open cadence, financial aid inquiry timing — our yield model weights these automatically, or you can override the weights yourself.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Act on the insight">
    Trigger a counselor task, send a personalized nudge, or export a prioritized call list. One click, auditable, reversible.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Before you build

<Warning>
  SuperAdmission accesses student records. Make sure your institution's data governance team has reviewed the [Data Processing Agreement](/legal/dpa) before connecting a production CRM.
</Warning>

A few things worth knowing up front:

- **Sandbox mode is always available.** Every account ships with a synthetic cohort of 2,000 mock applicants so you can build and test without touching live data.
- **We don't train on your data.** Your applicant records are used to serve your queries. Full stop.
- **Webhooks are one-way by default.** We push to you. Any write-back to your CRM is an explicit action your team initiates.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does the initial CRM sync take?" icon="clock">
    For most institutions, the first historical sync — pulling 2–3 cycles of applicant data — completes in 15 to 45 minutes depending on volume. After that, incremental syncs run every 4 hours by default, configurable down to 30 minutes on the Growth plan.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can we use SuperAdmission without a CRM?" icon="database">
    Yes. You can upload a CSV export from any system, and we'll map the fields interactively. It's not as real-time as a direct connection, but you'll have full access to cohort analysis and yield scoring from day one.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is this FERPA compliant?" icon="shield">
    SuperAdmission is designed for FERPA compliance. We act as a school official under legitimate educational interest, data is encrypted at rest and in transit, and we support data processing agreements for institutions that require them. See our [Security overview](/security) for the full picture.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if our yield model is already tuned?" icon="sliders">
    Good — bring it. You can upload a custom weight matrix via the API and the system will use your coefficients instead of the default ones. Our model is a starting point, not a constraint.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
