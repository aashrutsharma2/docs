## 2026 - May

<Card title="Applied for Startup India Seed Fund Scheme">
  31 May 2026 · Funding

  Submitted a formal application to the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS), a Government of India programme administered by DPIIT to provide early-stage capital to recognised startups building solutions with meaningful societal and economic impact. The application positions Superadmission within the edtech and AI infrastructure space, seeking support for proof-of-concept development and early prototype work on the core platform layer. If selected, the grant will accelerate the buildout of the admissions intelligence system and enable the team to move from validated prototype to a testable product in the hands of real users.
</Card>

## 2026 - April

<Card title="Attended YC Startup School - Bangalore">
  18 April 2026 · Partnership

  Attended Y Combinator's Startup School event in Bangalore, one of the most significant gatherings of early-stage founders in the Indian startup calendar. The day featured programming from YC partners and portfolio founders covering product thinking, go-to-market strategy, fundraising mechanics, and lessons from building at speed, delivered to a room of founders operating across sectors and stages. For Superadmission, the event was an opportunity to pressure-test thinking on product direction and positioning, draw comparisons with how similar problems are being approached globally, and build relationships with peers in the founder community navigating the same early-stage challenges.
</Card>

## 2026 - February - March

<Card title="Product Demo Live">
  12 March 2026 · Build

  Published the first externally accessible product demonstration of Superadmission, marking a significant internal milestone as the platform moved from closed development into the hands of real observers for the first time. The demo showcases the core application intelligence layer ( how Superadmission reads, evaluates, and guides the admissions process )  and is designed to communicate the product's value proposition to prospective users, early adopters, and potential partners without requiring a full onboarding. Releasing a live demo at this stage allows the team to gather structured feedback, validate core assumptions about usability and positioning, and build credibility with stakeholders ahead of a broader launch.
</Card>

<Card title="Phase 1 Frontend Developed">
  18 February 2026 · Build

  Completed the full development of the Phase 1 frontend for Superadmission, establishing the foundational user-facing layer of the platform. This phase covers the primary interaction surfaces including application flows, core navigation architecture, dashboard structure, and the initial UI framework that will carry the product through early user testing. Delivering this milestone closes out the design-to-build loop for the first phase of product development and shifts the team's focus toward backend integration, data layer connectivity, and preparing the platform for its first round of real-world usage with prospective applicants and institutions.
</Card>

<Card title="Superadmission Trademark Filed">
  2 February 2026 · Policy

  Filed for trademark registration of the Superadmission brand under applicable intellectual property statutes, formally securing the name and associated marks used in the delivery of the platform and its services. This filing is a foundational step in protecting the commercial identity of the company as it moves toward public launch  ensuring that the brand built through product development, marketing, and community engagement remains exclusively associated with the platform. Trademark protection also signals maturity and seriousness of intent to prospective partners, institutional clients, and investors evaluating the company at this stage.
</Card>

## 2026 - January

<Card title="Attended Startup India 10th Anniversary Event">
  16 January 2026 · Partnership

  Represented Superadmission at the Startup India 10th Anniversary flagship event, an exclusive, invite-only gathering held to mark a decade of the Government of India's landmark startup initiative, bringing together founders, investors, ministry officials, and ecosystem leaders from across the country. The event was addressed by Prime Minister Narendra Modi and featured substantive dialogue around the next chapter of India's innovation agenda, with particular emphasis on emerging technology, deep-tech infrastructure, and building globally competitive companies from India. Attending this event placed Superadmission within a curated cohort of startups recognised at a national level and provided direct access to senior policymakers, potential institutional partners, and investors aligned with the government's broader vision for the startup ecosystem.
</Card>

## 2025 - September

<Card title="Aidra LLP Incorporated">
  3 September 2025 · Team

  Formally incorporated Aidra LLP, the legal entity under which Superadmission is built and operated, marking the official founding of the company and the beginning of its existence as a structured commercial organisation. Incorporation establishes the legal and regulatory foundation required for all subsequent activity from entering contracts and opening banking relationships to filing for intellectual property rights, applying for government schemes, and onboarding team members under formal employment and partnership agreements. This date represents the starting point of the Superadmission journey as a company, and the commitment of the founding team to building a lasting institution in the admissions intelligence space.
</Card>
