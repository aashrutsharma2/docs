Superadmission is built by Aashrut Sharma and Unnati Vijay, two founders in their early twenties who spent two years in the field before building the platform.

The work started with College Cult a student admissions guidance service that worked directly with close to 5,000 students through their counselling cycles. Every conversation revealed the same pattern: students were not failing because of their rank. They were failing because the process was not designed to be navigated without help. That observation became the foundation for Superadmission.

Aashrut leads product, technology, and research. He designed and built the platform's core systems the full-stack application, the India Stack integrations and PraveshAI. 

Unnati leads operations, research and institutional partnerships. She designed the student-facing workflow and seat allocation engine. 

The research behind the platform spans 5,247 students, 218 institutions, and 18 states across two admission cycles. Superadmission is DPIIT-recognised under Startup India.
