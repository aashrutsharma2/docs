## The application layer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Profile carries automatically">
    Personal details, academic qualifications, and category information pre-filled from the verified profile. Exam scores auto-fetched where the counselling authority has integrated score feeds — NTA for JEE Main and NEET UG, for example.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Eligibility confirmed before choice-filling">
    Before the student sees a single institution to choose from, Pravesh AI checks their eligibility against each counselling's criteria in real time. What the student sees is a filtered, confirmed list — not a directory to search through and cross-check manually.
  </Step>
  <Step title="All participating counsellings visible in one place">
    Every counselling the student has registered for, every round status, every deadline — unified across one dashboard. No tab-switching. No missed updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Documents: verify once, accepted everywhere

Students currently submit documents an average of 4+ times per cycle. Each submission enters a separate verification queue. Each queue takes 5 to 15 days. The information being verified is identical every time.

On Superadmission, documents verified at profile formation carry a trust flag from their issuing source. Every participating counselling authority accepts that flag. The student does not re-submit. The authority does not re-verify.

## Proactive deadline intelligence

<img src="/images/samplescreenshot.jpg" alt="Deadline conflict alert interface" />

The system tracks the student's active counselling registrations and flags conflicts before they happen.

- Payment deadline 4 hours away — flagged
- Choice-filling window closing tomorrow — advance reminder sent
- Two acceptance windows overlapping — conflict detected and explained 48 hours before
- New category seat opened at a preferred institution — notified before the student would have thought to check

The shift is from a system that requires constant vigilance to one that provides it.

## What changes for counselling authorities

When students apply through Superadmission, authorities receive pre-validated intake data. The difference in operational load is significant:

| Current intake process | With Superadmission |
|---|---|
| Run document verification from scratch | Receive pre-verified student records |
| Manual eligibility checking for borderline cases | Machine-readable eligibility flags |
| High grievance volume from document rejections | Disputes drop — issues caught at profile formation |
| No view of what students are doing elsewhere | Aggregate ecosystem data within governed boundaries |

## The infrastructure point

Today's counselling portals are built for the concentrated burst — millions of students hitting a single server the moment results declare or a round opens. The outcome is predictable: slowdowns, crashes, and inaccessibility exactly when reliability matters most.

Superadmission is built on distributed public infrastructure. Load is spread across the architecture. The system stays functional at peak demand — not as a performance improvement but as a structural design choice.
