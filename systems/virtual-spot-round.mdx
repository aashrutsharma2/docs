A spot round is what happens after all scheduled counselling rounds have concluded and seats remain unfilled. Historically, this has meant students travelling to a physical venue, carrying demand drafts, standing in queues, and hoping the seat they wanted was not already gone by the time their turn came.

Superadmission's Virtual Spot Round removes all of that. The session is live, digital, and rank-gated — students participate from wherever they are, with full visibility into the queue and available seats in real time.

---

## Before the session opens

You know a spot round is coming before it starts. The pre-session screen shows you:

**The countdown** — exact days, hours, minutes, and seconds until the session opens. Not "check the website for updates."

**Your eligibility status** — Documents, category verification, UPI payment method, and any counselling-specific requirements like a medical fitness certificate. Everything is checked before the session begins, not at the gate. If something is missing, you have time to fix it.

**Your estimated queue position** — based on your rank among registered candidates in your category. You know roughly where you stand before you enter the session.

**Round Trend Insight** — Pravesh AI surfaces what actually happened in last year's equivalent round for your rank and category. Which branches filled fastest, which had seats remaining, what the realistic options looked like for someone at your rank. Not generic advice. Specific data from the previous cycle for your situation.

<Tip>
Physical spot rounds at some state counsellings have historically seen students travel overnight to venues, only to find that seats in their preferred branches filled within the first hour of the session opening. The virtual format means no one loses a seat to geography or a delayed train.
</Tip>

## During the live session

Once the session opens, the screen changes to the live environment.

**YOUR STATUS** shows your queue position, how many students are ahead of you, how many are behind you, and an estimated wait time. Your documents, UPI status, and eligibility are confirmed as green. The system knows you are ready before it is your turn.

**LIVE SEAT BOARD** shows every available seat in real time — branch, institute, current availability count, last year's closing rank for your category, and a chance indicator. This updates continuously as students ahead of you make their selections. You are not deciding based on a static snapshot. You are seeing what is actually available right now.

**QUEUE LIVE** shows the students immediately ahead of you — who is processing, who is next. When your position reaches Next, the seat selection modal opens automatically.

---

## When it is your turn

The moment your turn arrives, a timer starts — 8 minutes to make your selection. The modal shows every seat available to you, with the institute, branch, category, annual fee, and previous year's closing rank for each option. A Details link opens the full institute profile if you need it.

You select a seat. Or you pass — up to twice — if you want to wait and see what remains after the students directly ahead of you have chosen.

Passing is a real option because the system accounts for the fact that seat selection under time pressure is a genuine decision, not just a form submission.

Once you select a seat, payment flows through UPI and confirmation is issued. The allotment letter is available immediately for download and WhatsApp sharing.

---

## What the session looks like in numbers

Every live session shows a running count: how many students have been processed and how many are registered for that session. You can see the session's pace throughout. If the queue is moving faster than expected, you know. If it is slower, you know that too.

The session has a defined window — a start time and an end time. Students who do not participate or whose turn does not come before the window closes are handled according to the counselling authority's configured rules.
